RehearsalTracker

RehearsalTracker is a full-stack web application I built to help stage management staff at arts organizations keep rehearsal information collected in a digital format and in a centralized location. The app also gives stage management the ability to send rehearsal information to participants via email directly from the main page. Some of the technologies used to build Rehearsal Tracker were Modern JavaScript, React, Redux, CSS, HTML5, PostgreSQL, Postico, MaterialUI, EmailJS, SweetAlert, NodeJS and Express.
